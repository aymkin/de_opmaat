Title: In de supermarkt
Language: Dutch (nl-NL)
Level: A2-B1
Speech Rate: Slow (for language learning)

VOICE SETTINGS:
- Verkoper: Male voice, Dutch accent, slow and clear pronunciation
- Anna: Female voice, Dutch accent, slow and clear pronunciation
